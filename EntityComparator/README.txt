Problem Statement:
=================================
You have online debt recovery form with loanee name, Amount and a document of loan agreement.
The document is in text format with these details in description.
We need to confirm the name in text is same as in the online form also, else, raise an error.
A manual checking is required to open the document and confirm the details.

Solution
=================================
Use an NER model to capture name, amount and other entities from the text compare them.
